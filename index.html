How to Cook Pure Pishori Rice

1. Rinse the rice thoroughly to remove excess starch.
2. Use a 1:2 rice to water ratio.
3. Bring to a boil, then simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
4. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
